    Program Schedule

    We plan to apply for an associated ICPR workshop. The workshop will be a half-day event. We will have two invited talks, a poster session, and winner presentation for two tasks.

    Time (EDT) Content
    08:30-08:40 Opening Remarks
    08:40-09:20 Invited Talk1
    09:20-10:00 Dataset, Challenge Tasks, and Results
    10:00-10:30 Poster session
    10:30-11:00 Invited Talk2
    11:00-12:00 Winner presentation
    12:00-12:10 Award Session & Closing Remarks

    Besides, if the workshop is in-person, we can organize it in a regular and interactive way with invited talks and winner presentations in the designated place of ICPR2022. If the workshop is partially or fully virtual, we will use online platforms to support attendance. We will release introduction materials, including a 15-minute video and the corresponding slides for each task in advance, so that attendees can have a better knowledge of our workshop. Videos and slides will be uploaded to our homepage. During the workshop, we will arrange online talks and interactive sessions via Zoom. If allowed, we will also release the record of the workshop on YouTube for further reference.
